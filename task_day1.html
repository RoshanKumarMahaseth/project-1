Narendra Modi is the current Prime Minister of India, having first assumed office in May 2014. Born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat, he began his political career as a member of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) and later joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).
